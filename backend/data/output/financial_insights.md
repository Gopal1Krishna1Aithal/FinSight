### Actionable Recommendations

1. **Implement a cash flow forecasting system**: To better manage cash flow, the company should implement a cash flow forecasting system to predict future inflows and outflows.
2. **Reduce unnecessary ATM withdrawals**: The company should monitor ATM withdrawals closely and implement measures to reduce unnecessary withdrawals, such as using digital payment methods or setting up a cashless policy.
3. **Optimize Payroll expenses**: The company should review its Payroll expenses to ensure that they are optimized and not excessive. This may involve renegotiating contracts with employees or implementing cost-saving measures.
4. **Increase income from high-margin categories**: The company should focus on increasing income from high-margin categories, such as Software & IT, to improve profitability and reduce dependence on low-margin categories, such as E-Commerce & Retail.
